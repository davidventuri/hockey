Craig's List

TSN Scout Craig Button produced 10 monthly rankings (September-June) this season featuring the top players eligible for the 2012 NHL Entry Draft. Craig's monthly rankings represent his individual assessment of where players are slotted in the NHL draft. They will be posted in addition to Bob McKenzie's consensus NHL rankings, which is compiled four times a year. Bob's rankings represent the consensus NHL team view of where players are slotted in the NHL draft.

You can follow Craig on Twitter at @CraigJButton and Bob at @TSNBobMcKenzie.

Updated: June 11, 2012

Goaltending statistics are as follows: games, wins, goals-against average, save percentage and shutouts. The shot header applies to the goaltender's glove hand.

The 2012 NHL Entry Draft is scheduled for June 22-23 in Pittsburgh. The following 2012 first round picks have been traded:

Colorado to Washington (G Semyon Varlamov from Washington to Colorado for the Avalanche's 2012 first round pick and a second round pick in either 2012 or 2013).

Detroit to Tampa Bay (D Kyle Quincey to Detroit for the Red Wings 2012 first round pick and defenceman Sebastien Piche).

Los Angeles to Columbus (F Jeff Carter to Los Angeles for a conditional first round pick and defenceman Jack Johnson. The Blue Jackets have their choice of either Los Angeles' 1st rounder in 2012 or 2013, since the Kings make the 2012 Stanley Cup Playoffs).

Nashville to Buffalo (F Paul Gaustad and a fourth-round pick to Nashville for the Predators 2012 first round pick).

The Edmonton Oilers selected Nail Yakupov with the first overall pick of the 2012 NHL Draft.
